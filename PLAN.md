# Busan Vibes - Development Plan

## Project Overview

Busan Vibes is a multilingual tourism platform designed for international visitors exploring Busan, South Korea.

The website introduces:

* Tourist Attractions
* Restaurants
* Cafes
* Local Foods
* Instagram-worthy Places
* Festivals & Events
* Travel Tips
* Interactive Maps

Supported Languages:

* Korean (ko)
* English (en)
* French (fr)

---

## Target Audience

### Primary

* English-speaking tourists
* French-speaking tourists
* First-time visitors to Busan

### Secondary

* Korean domestic travelers
* Digital nomads
* Content creators
